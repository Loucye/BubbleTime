Welcome

Welcome to Bubble Time a site for bubble tea lovers or just for the curious. You will find here recipes to do your own bubble tea, some history on this popular trend, information for your health as well as a map to find the best bubble tea in town !

What this website is about?

If you wish to know more about bubble tea and how it came to be, we have a page History that will tell you all you need to know about this popular trend, we can also show you the most atypical forms in which bubble tea can be eaten in our page Atypical Bubble Tea
